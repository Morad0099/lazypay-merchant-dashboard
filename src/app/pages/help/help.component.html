Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a DoronPay merchant account?
To create a DoronPay merchant account, visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Complete the registration form with your business details, verify your email, and submit the required documentation for verification. Once approved, you'll receive your merchant credentials to start accepting payments.
What payment methods does DoronPay support?
DoronPay supports a wide range of payment methods including mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, card payments, and cryptocurrencies (USDT and BTC). This allows your customers to use their preferred payment option when making transactions.
How do I integrate DoronPay with my website?
Integrating DoronPay is simple. Once you have your API credentials, you can use our REST APIs to implement payment functionality on your website. We provide comprehensive API documentation, client libraries for popular programming languages, and plugins for major e-commerce platforms to make integration seamless.
What are the transaction fees?
Our transaction fees vary based on your payment volume and the payment method used. Generally, mobile money transactions have a fee of 1-2%, bank transfers 1.5%, and card payments 2.5%. High-volume merchants are eligible for custom rates. Please contact our sales team for a personalized quote based on your business needs.
How long does it take to receive payments?
Mobile money transactions are typically processed in real-time. Bank transfers are processed within 24 hours during business days. Settlement to your account follows your configured settlement schedule, which can be daily, weekly, or monthly. Premium merchants can access instant settlements for an additional fee.
How do I set up USSD payments?
To set up USSD payments, log into your merchant dashboard, navigate to the Hub section, and click "Update Wallet Details Icon." Enter your USSD shortcode, provide a callback URL for payment notifications, enable USSD Payments using the checkbox, and save your configuration by clicking "Update Settings." No technical integration is required.
Is DoronPay secure for processing payments?
Yes, DoronPay employs industry-standard security measures including end-to-end encryption, PCI DSS compliance, two-factor authentication, and regular security audits. All sensitive data is encrypted and securely stored. We also monitor transactions in real-time to detect and prevent fraudulent activities, ensuring both merchants and customers are protected.
How do I view my transaction history?
You can view your complete transaction history by logging into your merchant dashboard. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of all transactions, including successful payments, failed attempts, and pending transactions. You can filter by date range, payment method, transaction status, and export reports in various formats for your accounting needs.

If you couldn't find what you were looking for, please contact our support team.
